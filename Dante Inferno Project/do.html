"Inferno Canto XII"

Do self relfection

Take the opportunity to reflect on your own life and consider whether there are sins you need to repent for. A journey is also a symbolic representation of the soul's purification and redemption.

"Inferno Canto XIII"

Appreciate the Literary and Historical References

Dante's Inferno is rich in literary and historical references. Take the time to appreciate the depth of the work and the various layers of meaning.
