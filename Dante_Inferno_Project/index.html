Preface

This will be Virgils Do’s and Dont’s website brochure for those traveling through hell. After Dante visited, Virgil realized that it would be extremely helpful for the next tourist of hell to know what to expect and no what not to do ahead of time - like don’t faint every canto. In Essence, my project for Dante’s Inferno is a brief parody of the character and the challenges he goes through, through the medium of HTML, CSS, and hopefully not too much javascript.
