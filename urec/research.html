UREC's internal research arm (research & consulting) consists of 2-3 teams of undergraduate research associates especially passionate about learning more about the real estate industry. Research associates are carefully selected out of a pool of 100+ applicants and also complete a 10-week real estate finance training program before being assigned to a team. Each team will be led by 1 to 2 experienced project managers who have prior internship experience in the industry.
